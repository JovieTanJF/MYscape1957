 Cuisine 

Penang

Oyster Omelette

The freshness of the oysters used in this meal sets it apart from others. To give the oysters a crisp yet soft feel, fried rice flour batter and eggs are combined with them. You also receive chili, which is a bit sweeter than usual, with the omelette.

Cendol

This lovely bowl of shaved ice will make you smile. It is paired with red beans, fresh coconut milk, chewy green rice flour jelly (chendol), and gula Melaka (brown sugar) syrup. It may be found practically anywhere in Penang's streets.

Penang Laksa

Authentic Penang dishes include this acidic hot soup with plump white noodles and sprinkling of tuna. It has a unique smell. It's worth a try even if you don't think it's worth it because of the sweet-sour taste.
